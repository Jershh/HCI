Queen's Bedroom

A double room can accommodate up to four people because of its two double beds, suitable for two friends or business partners, and double rooms are better for families with young children.

Junior Suite

A junior suite is similar to a standard suite but smaller in size. It is less likely to comprise separate rooms but will have a layout that demarcates the sitting and sleeping areas.

Suite

Suites accommodate groups of people who like to have their personal space but still stay together. Suites often include a kitchenette, dining area, sofa bed, TV, and separate bathrooms.
